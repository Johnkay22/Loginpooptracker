How to Play

1. Enter your bet amount

2. Click "Bet" to start the game

3. Send Super Turd to the moon & watch the multiplier increase

4. Click "Cash Out" before Super Turd crashes otherwise you lose your bet

5. If you cash out in time, you win your bet multiplied by the current multiplier!
